The AI/ML Lab is a research group headed by Prof. Shitala Prasad , dedicated to advancing the theory and practice of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, with a strong emphasis on developing reliable, interpretable, and real-world AI systems. The lab conducts interdisciplinary research spanning AI-driven understanding of marine biology, autonomous systems using UAVs and AUVs, and data-driven intelligence for complex real-world environments.
